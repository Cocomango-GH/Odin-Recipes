Strawberry Smoothie

Start your morning with this Delicioussmoothie to jump start your morning.

Ingredients

  • 8 strawberries, hulled
  • 1/2 cup skim milk
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 6 cubes ice, crushed

Directions

  1. Combine strawberries, milk, yogurt, sugar, and vanilla in a blender. Add ice and blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
