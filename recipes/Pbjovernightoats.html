Overnight Light PB&J Oats

Delicious bowl of warm overnight oats

This grab and go breakfast is perfect for busy mornings!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1/4 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1/4 cup rolled oats
  • 2 tablespoons powdered peanut butter (such as PB2Â®)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons chia seeds
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar

Directions

  1. Mix almond milk, raspberries, rolled oats, powdered peanut butter, chia seeds, and sugar together in a container. Cover and refrigerate until oats are soft, 8 hours to overnight.
